# New Staff Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the Service Org! This guide walks you through everything you need to get set up and productive in your first week.

---

## Pre-Start Checklist (HR / IT)

- [ ] Employee record created in HR system
- [ ] Laptop provisioned and shipped / ready for pickup
- [ ] Corporate email address created (`firstname.lastname@example.com`)
- [ ] Added to company Slack workspace
- [ ] Added to relevant distribution lists

---

## Day 1 — Account & Access Setup

### 1. Activate Your Corporate Google Account
1. Navigate to [accounts.google.com](https://accounts.google.com)
2. Sign in with your `@example.com` credentials provided by IT
3. Set a strong password and enroll in 2-step verification

### 2. Log In to the Service Portal
1. Go to [https://service-portal.example.com](https://service-portal.example.com)
2. Click **Sign in with Google** and use your corporate account
3. Confirm your name and avatar appear in the sidebar

### 3. Request Tool Access
Submit access requests for the following systems through the IT Help Desk (helpdesk@example.com):

| Tool | Access Level | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Jira | Project Member | Required for case tracking |
| Confluence | Editor | Internal documentation |
| PagerDuty | Responder | On-call rotations |
| Google Cloud Console | Viewer | Monitoring dashboards |

---

## Day 2–3 — Orientation Training

### Required Trainings (complete within first week)
1. **Security Awareness** — link sent via email from security@example.com
2. **Data Handling Policy** — available in Confluence > HR Policies
3. **Service Org Processes** — 60-min session with your team lead

### Meet Your Team
Schedule 30-minute intro calls with:
- Your direct manager
- At least two peers in your department
- One member from an adjacent team (e.g., if you're in Technical, meet someone from Operations)

---

## Week 1 — First Tasks

1. Shadow an active case from open to resolution
2. Read the [Escalation Policy](escalation-policy.md) thoroughly
3. Join the `#service-org` and `#incidents` Slack channels
4. Add yourself to the staff directory in `data/config.json` (PR required)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Who do I contact if I can't log in to the portal?**  
A: Email IT Help Desk at helpdesk@example.com or ping `#it-support` on Slack.

**Q: How do I get added to the on-call rotation?**  
A: Talk to your manager after completing your first 30 days.

**Q: Where do I report a security incident?**  
A: Immediately contact security@example.com and your manager. Do not attempt to handle it alone.
